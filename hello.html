Greetings

Greeting is an act of communication in which human beings intentionally make their presence known to each other, to show attention to, and to suggest a type of relationship (usually cordial) or social status (formal or informal) between individuals or groups of people coming in contact with each other.

Greetings

Greetings are sometimes used just prior to a conversation or to greet in passing, such as on a sidewalk or trail. While greeting customs are highly culture- and situation - specific and may change within a culture depending on social status and relationship, they exist in all known human cultures.
Greetings can be expressed both audibly and physically, and often involve a combination of the two.
